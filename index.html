Robert Laugwitz

I am an Assistant Professor at the School of Mathematical Sciences, University of Nottingham.

Before coming to Nottingham in 2019, I was a Triennial Assistant Professor at the Department of Mathematics, Rutgers University (2016-2019) and a Research Associate at the University of East Anglia (2015-2016 and Summer 2019). I completed my PhD at University of Oxford with Prof Kobi Kremnizer (2015) .

This website is a personal research website and the content posted here does not represent the University of Nottingham. For my official university page, please see my Nottingham Profile. For information on teaching, please use the University's Moodle.
